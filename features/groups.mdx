## Overview

Groups are the foundation of EquiShare. Each group represents a shared expense context - whether it's a vacation with friends, household expenses with roommates, or a project with colleagues. Groups help you organize who participated in expenses and how costs should be split.

## Creating a Group

To create a new group:

1. Click the **Create New Group** button on your dashboard
2. Enter a descriptive group name (e.g., "Weekend Trip", "House Expenses")
3. Optionally add people to the group during creation
4. Click **Create Group**

<Note>
  You can add people later, so don't worry if you don't know everyone who'll participate yet.
</Note>

### Adding People

When creating a group, you can add people by:

- Typing each person's name
- Pressing Enter or clicking the plus icon to add them
- Removing people by clicking the X on their badge

## Managing Group Members

### Adding People to an Existing Group

Once your group is created, you can add more people:

1. Navigate to your group page
2. Find the **People** section
3. Enter the person's name in the "Add New Person" field
4. Click the user plus icon to add them

### Removing People

To remove someone from a group:

1. Hover over the person's name in the People section
2. Click the minus icon that appears
3. Confirm the deletion

<Warning>
  You can only remove people who haven't participated in any expenses or settlements. If someone has expense history, you'll need to settle or delete those expenses first.
</Warning>

## Group Access Control

EquiShare has two types of group access:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Group Owner" icon="crown">
    The user who created the group. Owners can:
    - Delete the entire group
    - Remove members
    - Generate invite links
    - Manage all expenses and settlements
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Group Members" icon="user">
    Users invited to join the group. Members can:
    - View all expenses and settlements
    - Add and delete expenses
    - Mark settlements as paid
    - Leave the group at any time
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Inviting Members

Group owners can invite others by generating invite links. See the [Invites](/features/invites) page for details.

### Leaving a Group

Members (but not owners) can leave a group at any time:

1. Navigate to the group page
2. Click the **Leave Group** option
3. Confirm your departure

<Info>
  As the group owner, you cannot leave your own group - you can only delete it. This ensures groups always have an owner who can manage them.
</Info>

## Viewing Group Data

### Group Dashboard

Each group has a comprehensive dashboard showing:

- **People**: All participants in the group
- **Expenses**: Complete list of shared expenses
- **Settlements**: Current balances and who owes whom

The dashboard provides a real-time overview of the group's financial status.

### Understanding Group Balances

Group balances are automatically calculated based on:

- Who paid for each expense
- How expenses were split among participants
- Which settlements have been marked as paid

The system uses a minimal transaction algorithm to reduce the number of payments needed to settle all debts.

## Exporting Group Data

You can export complete group data for your records or external use.

### Export Formats

EquiShare supports two export formats:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="CSV Export">
    Download a spreadsheet-compatible CSV file containing:
    - All group expenses with dates and amounts
    - Split details showing who owes what
    - Settlement history
    - Current outstanding balances
    
    Perfect for importing into Excel, Google Sheets, or other financial tools.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="PDF Export">
    Generate a formatted PDF document with:
    - Group summary information
    - Complete expense listing
    - Settlement breakdown
    - Visual formatting for easy printing
    
    Ideal for record-keeping or sharing with accountants.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### How to Export

1. Navigate to your group page
2. Click the **download icon** in the top section
3. Choose your preferred format (CSV or PDF)
4. The file downloads automatically

<Tip>
  Export your group data before settling all expenses to maintain a complete financial record.
</Tip>

## Deleting a Group

Only group owners can delete a group:

1. Navigate to the group page
2. Find the delete option (usually in group settings)
3. Confirm the deletion

<Warning>
  Deleting a group permanently removes all expenses, settlements, and people associated with it. This action cannot be undone. Consider exporting your data first.
</Warning>

## Best Practices

### Naming Groups

- Use descriptive names that indicate the purpose: "Bali Vacation 2024" instead of just "Trip"
- Include timeframes for temporary groups: "March House Bills"
- Keep names concise but meaningful

### Managing People

- Add people before creating expenses to ensure everyone is available when splitting costs
- Use consistent names for the same person across different groups
- Remove people who won't participate before they're included in expenses

### Organization

- Create separate groups for different contexts (don't mix vacation and household expenses)
- Archive or delete groups once all expenses are settled
- Regularly export data for groups with many transactions

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Remove a Person

If you can't remove someone, they're likely referenced in expenses or settlements. You'll need to:

1. Check the Expenses section for their involvement
2. Either delete those expenses or settle them
3. Try removing the person again

### Can't Find My Group

Make sure you're looking at groups where you're either:
- The owner (you created it)
- A member (someone invited you)

Groups you've left or were removed from won't appear in your list.

### Export Not Working

If exports fail:
- Check your browser's download settings
- Ensure popup blockers aren't interfering
- Try a different export format
- Check that the group has data to export
