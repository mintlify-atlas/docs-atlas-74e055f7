## What is EquiShare?

EquiShare is a modern expense splitting application that helps groups of people track shared expenses and balances. Whether you're planning a trip with friends, managing household expenses with roommates, or splitting bills with colleagues, EquiShare makes it easy to track who paid what and who owes whom.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Group Management" icon="users">
    Create unlimited groups for different expense-sharing scenarios:
    - Weekend trips and vacations
    - Household and roommate expenses
    - Team events and activities
    - Family gatherings
    
    Each group can have multiple members and non-registered participants (people).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Easy Expense Tracking" icon="receipt">
    Add expenses quickly with flexible splitting options:
    - **Equal Split**: Divide expenses evenly among participants
    - **Percentage Split**: Split by custom percentages
    - **Exact Amount**: Specify exact amounts for each person
    - **Shares**: Split by shares or units
    
    Track who paid and who participated in each expense.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Smart Settlements" icon="chart-simple">
    EquiShare automatically calculates the optimal way to settle debts:
    - Minimizes the number of transactions needed
    - Real-time balance calculations
    - Clear visualization of who owes whom
    - Mark settlements as complete when paid
    - Export settlement summaries as PDF or images
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Collaborative Features" icon="share-nodes">
    Work together with your group members:
    - Invite members via email
    - Real-time updates for all group members
    - Add and manage non-registered participants
    - Leave groups when no longer needed
    - Group owners have full management control
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Built With Modern Technology

EquiShare is built using the **T3 Stack**, a powerful combination of modern web technologies:

- **Next.js 15** - React framework with App Router
- **NextAuth.js 5** - Secure authentication
- **Prisma** - Type-safe database ORM
- **tRPC** - End-to-end typesafe APIs
- **Tailwind CSS** - Utility-first styling
- **TypeScript** - Type safety throughout

<Info>
  EquiShare is designed with privacy in mind. Your expense data belongs to you and is securely stored.
</Info>
