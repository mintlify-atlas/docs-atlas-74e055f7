## Welcome to EquiShare!

This guide will walk you through everything you need to start splitting expenses with your friends, family, or colleagues. In just a few minutes, you'll:

- Sign in with Google
- Create your first group
- Add members
- Record an expense
- View settlement suggestions

<Note>
  Before you begin, make sure you have a Google account for authentication.
</Note>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In with Google">
    Navigate to your EquiShare instance and click the **"Continue with Google"** button to sign in.
    
    ```tsx
    // EquiShare uses NextAuth.js for secure authentication
    await signIn("google", { callbackUrl: "/groups" });
    ```
    
    After successful authentication, you'll be redirected to your Groups dashboard.
    
    <Info>
      EquiShare uses NextAuth.js 5 with Google OAuth for secure, passwordless authentication. Your credentials are never stored on EquiShare servers.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your First Group">
    On the Groups page, click the **"Create New Group"** button to open the group creation dialog.
    
    **Fill in the group details:**
    
    1. **Group Name** (required): Give your group a descriptive name
       - Examples: "Weekend Trip to Miami", "Apartment 4B", "Office Lunch Club"
    
    2. **Add People** (optional): Add non-registered participants to your group
       - Type a name and press Enter or click the + button
       - These are people who don't have EquiShare accounts but participated in expenses
       - You can also add them later
    
    ```tsx
    // Groups can be created with initial people
    const group = await api.group.create.mutate({
      name: "Weekend Trip to Miami",
      people: ["Alex", "Jordan", "Sam"]
    });
    ```
    
    Click **"Create Group"** when ready. You'll be automatically taken to your new group's page.
    
    <Tip>
      Start with just a group name if you're not sure who will participate yet. You can always add people later!
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite Group Members (Optional)">
    To collaborate with other EquiShare users, invite them to your group:
    
    1. Click the **"Invite Members"** button on your group page
    2. Enter the email addresses of people you want to invite
    3. They'll receive an invitation link to join your group
    
    **Group Members vs. People:**
    - **Members**: Registered EquiShare users who can view and add expenses
    - **People**: Non-registered participants you can assign to expenses
    
    <Info>
      Only the group owner can invite members and delete the group. All members can add expenses and people.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Your First Expense">
    Now let's record an expense. Click the **"Add Expense"** button.
    
    **Step 1: Expense Details**
    
    1. **Description**: What was the expense for?
       - Example: "Hotel room for 3 nights"
    
    2. **Amount**: How much was spent?
       - Example: 450.00
    
    3. **Paid by**: Who paid for this expense?
       - Select from your group members or people
    
    4. **Split between**: Who should share this expense?
       - Select all participants who should pay their share
       - You can select multiple people
    
    Click **"Continue"** to proceed to split configuration.
    
    **Step 2: Configure the Split**
    
    Choose how to split the expense:
    
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Equal Split">
        The amount is divided equally among all selected participants.
        
        ```tsx
        // Example: $450 split equally among 3 people = $150 each
        splitMode: "equal"
        ```
        
        This is the default and most common option.
      </Tab>
      
      <Tab title="Percentage Split">
        Assign a percentage to each participant (must total 100%).
        
        ```tsx
        // Example: $450 split 50%, 30%, 20%
        splitMode: "percentage"
        shareValues: {
          "person-1-id": 50,
          "person-2-id": 30,
          "person-3-id": 20
        }
        ```
      </Tab>
      
      <Tab title="Exact Amount">
        Specify the exact amount each participant should pay.
        
        ```tsx
        // Example: Custom amounts that total $450
        splitMode: "exact"
        shareValues: {
          "person-1-id": 200,
          "person-2-id": 150,
          "person-3-id": 100
        }
        ```
      </Tab>
      
      <Tab title="Shares">
        Assign shares/units to each participant.
        
        ```tsx
        // Example: 3 shares, 2 shares, 1 share (ratio 3:2:1)
        splitMode: "shares"
        shareValues: {
          "person-1-id": 3,
          "person-2-id": 2,
          "person-3-id": 1
        }
        ```
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
    
    Click **"Add Expense"** to save.
    
    <Check>
      Your expense is now recorded! EquiShare automatically calculates balances and settlement suggestions.
    </Check>
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Balances and Settlements">
    After adding expenses, EquiShare shows you:
    
    **Group Summary (Top of page)**
    - Total expenses in the group
    - Number of expenses recorded
    - Quick balance overview
    
    **Expenses List**
    - All expenses with descriptions, amounts, and splits
    - Who paid and who participated
    - Individual shares for each person
    
    **Settlements Section**
    
    This is where the magic happens! EquiShare calculates the optimal way to settle all debts:
    
    ```tsx
    // EquiShare minimizes transactions using smart algorithms
    // Example: If A owes B $50 and B owes C $50, 
    // it simplifies to: A pays C $50
    ```
    
    Each settlement shows:
    - **From**: Who needs to pay
    - **To**: Who should receive payment
    - **Amount**: How much to transfer
    
    <Tip>
      Click the checkmark button to mark a settlement as complete once the money has been transferred.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Mark Settlements as Complete">
    When someone pays their debt:
    
    1. Find the settlement in the Settlements list
    2. Click the **checkmark** or **"Settle"** button
    3. The settlement is marked as paid
    4. EquiShare recalculates remaining settlements automatically
    
    You can also use **"Settle All"** to mark all current settlements as complete at once.
    
    <Warning>
      Only mark settlements as complete after the actual money transfer happens. This ensures your balances stay accurate.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Workflows

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Split a Restaurant Bill" icon="utensils">
    1. Create a group for the dinner (e.g., "Friday Dinner")
    2. Add people who attended
    3. Add expense for the total bill
    4. Choose "equal" split if everyone pays the same
    5. Or use "exact" if people ordered different amounts
    6. View who owes the person who paid
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Track Roommate Expenses" icon="house">
    1. Create a group (e.g., "Apartment 4B")
    2. Invite your roommates as members
    3. Add recurring expenses (rent, utilities, groceries)
    4. Use different split modes based on usage
    5. Check balances monthly
    6. Settle up at the end of the month
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Plan a Group Trip" icon="plane">
    1. Create a group for the trip
    2. Invite all travelers as members
    3. Add expenses as they happen (flights, hotels, meals, activities)
    4. Different people can pay for different things
    5. At trip end, view settlement summary
    6. Everyone pays their share to settle up
    7. Export final summary for records
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
